PET TRACKER

Web application for tracking the real time location of your pet

Features

Sign Up:

Create an account and set it up to manage and locate your pets.

Create your pets:

Save your pet's information: birthaday, hair color, race, etc .

Real-time location:

Locate your pet in the map at any time just pressing a button.

About

Why we did it?

For some people, pets are currently one important aspect of their life. As pets continue becoming more and more popular, more humans want to have pets. However, having a risk of losing them supposes a problem for the owners.
